# Review: Marketing

## Preconditions
- Read `PRD.yml`, `roadmap.yml`, `_contract.md`
- Identify environment (local/staging/production)

## Review Areas

### Brand Identity
- Color palette, typography, logo consistency
- Tone and voice alignment
- Score: 1-10

### Messaging
- Tagline and value prop clarity
- Persona-specific messaging
- Cross-page consistency

### SEO
- Meta titles/descriptions
- Header hierarchy (H1/H2/H3)
- URL structure, alt tags
- Core Web Vitals

### Content Quality
- Readability and grammar
- Formatting and structure
- Engagement potential (scanability, CTAs)

### Social Proof
- Testimonials, case studies
- Client logos, certifications
- Trust indicators

### Channel Readiness
- Paid ads: landing pages, tracking
- Social: OpenGraph/Twitter cards
- Email: newsletter capture

## Output
- Marketing readiness score (1-100)
- Top 5 priorities
- Quick wins (<1 day)
- Record issues in `.seedgpt/bugs/`, `.seedgpt/features/`, or `.seedgpt/minor-improvements/`
- Commit updates to `main`

See: `_contract.md`
